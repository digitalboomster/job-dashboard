    § UK visa reality check

    Your MSc at Loughborough finished in 2024 — the UK Graduate Visa gives 2 years of post-study work rights, so unless yours has lapsed, you can take non-sponsoring London roles (InstaDeep, many startups). If it’s expired, filter hard for sponsoring employers: Spotify, ARM, DeepMind, Wise, Revolut, AstraZeneca, Microsoft UK, Amazon UK.

    The £41,700 Skilled Worker threshold in 2026 is above most junior ML bands — so for sponsored roles you need to come in at mid level or above, which given 1 yr experience is a real push.

    § Sharpening the odds

    To move stretch roles into medium over 6–12 months: ship one RL project end-to-end publicly (blog, GH, or preprint), contribute to CleanRL / Stable-Baselines3 / Prime Intellect’s stack, and target research residencies that don’t require a PhD — Cohere, AI2, and past Anthropic residencies have taken non-PhD candidates with strong portfolios.

    Network-wise: Zindi leaderboards and Data Science Nigeria are how Lagos ML teams quietly hire. Two strong competition placements beats most cold applications.
